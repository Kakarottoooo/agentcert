# Scoring

AgentCert scoring starts at 100 and applies deterministic penalties:

- critical: -45
- high: -20
- medium: -10
- low: -3

Critical violations force `Not certified`.

Levels:

- Platinum: 95+
- Gold: 90+
- Silver: 80+
- Bronze: 70+
- Not certified: critical violation or low score

Scores are explainable through violations and scorer evidence.
